About Me

Problem solving. Data wrangling. Story telling. Learning.

Programming has been a hobby for some time. It could be a news story or a conversation that gets me asking questions and thinking about how I could code an answer. I taught math for several years and my interest in programming lead to an opportunity to teach courses JavaScript, Python and Java. I have always been a numbers guy and the problems I like to solve often involve crunching the numbers. I love a good story and I have a lot of fun trying to find the stories that data can tell ... and its even better if that story includes a great data visualization. I would like to continue to solve problems with code as a full-time developer. I am currently working to complete the JavaScript learning path at Elevenfifty Academy.
